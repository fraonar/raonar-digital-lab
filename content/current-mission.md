The long-term vision of this laboratory is to completely bridge the gap between high-fidelity neural interface research and consumer-accessible medical assistive devices. By building in the open and releasing fully open-source hardware, firmware, and dataset benchmarks, we hope to accelerate global collaborative breakthroughs in bionics and neuro-prosthetics.
